NGBSE Report Template – textual guidance
Sections:
1. BLUF
2. Key Findings
3. Risk Assessment
4. Confidence & Evidence Quality
5. Blindspot Analysis (Coverage/Recency/Confidence gaps)
6. Forecast & Scenarios (Most likely 1–3 months; Alternative; Wildcard)
7. Recommendations / Action Items
8. Appendix (Citations, Assets, Raw Evidence references)
9. Analyst Review & Sign-off: Name, Date, Conclusion (agree/disagree), Signature
